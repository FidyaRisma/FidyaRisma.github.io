Potential Fishing Zone (PFZ) Parameters Map
in the Southern Coastal of Java Island

This project was developed to create a satellite data platform for sustainable ocean environment observation based on Challenge 7 (Sustainably expand the Global Ocean Observing System) and Challenge 8 (Create a digital representation of the ocean) of Ocean Decade 10 Challenges. The platform was built for sustainable fisheries by integrating various remote sensing data. The data available in this GEE Apps runs from 2022/01/01 until present.
